BODY BUILDING

Bodybuilding is the use of progressive resistance exercise to control and develop one's musculature (muscle building) by muscle hypertrophy for aesthetic purposes.

MUSCLE GAIN

Building muscle requires a positive energy balance, which means that you must take in more calories than you burn.

WEIGHT LOSS

Weight loss, in the context of medicine, health, or physical fitness, refers to a reduction of the total body mass, by a mean loss of fluid, body fat (adipose tissue), or lean mass.
